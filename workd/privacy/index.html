Workd Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

SNAPPS ENGINEERING LTD. built the Workd app. This policy explains how we handle information when you use Workd.

Information Collection and Use

Workd stores your workout and related data on your device. We do not operate a server that receives or hosts your workout content.

If you enable iCloud features and are signed in to iCloud, Apple may sync that app data through your personal iCloud account according to Apple’s terms and settings. That sync is between your devices and Apple’s iCloud service—we do not have access to your iCloud data.

We cannot access your data: we do not receive, store, or host your workouts, favorites, or other app content on our systems. What you save stays on your devices and, if you enable it, in your personal iCloud account under Apple’s control—we have no copy of it, no database of it, and no way to view or retrieve it.

Log Data

If an app error occurs, diagnostic information may be available through Apple platform logs. We do not run our own external tracking system for personal profiling in Workd.

Cookies

Workd is a native iOS app and does not use browser cookies.

Service Providers

Workd does not use third-party analytics, ad networks, or SDK-based data processors for app operation. Optional sync uses Apple’s iCloud, which is governed by your relationship with Apple—not by us as a separate data processor of your workout content.

Security

Your primary data stays on your device. If you use iCloud, Apple’s security and account protections apply to data in your iCloud account.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates are posted on this page with a revised effective date.
