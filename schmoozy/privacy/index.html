Schmoozy Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 16, 2026

SNAPPS ENGINEERING LTD. built the Schmoozy app. This policy explains how we handle information when you use Schmoozy.

No Data Collection

Schmoozy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All user data — including contacts, cards, and preferences — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

No Tracking

Schmoozy does not track users. There are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no third-party tracking services, and no cookies. We do not build user profiles or share data with anyone.

Device Permissions

Schmoozy may request access to your camera (for QR code scanning) and contacts (for contact sharing). This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Data Encryption

User data stored on your device is encrypted using AES-256-GCM, an industry-standard encryption algorithm, via Apple’s CryptoKit framework.

No Third-Party Services

Schmoozy does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data processing services.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. All sensitive data is encrypted at rest on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates are posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at snappsengineering@gmail.com.
