Schmoozy Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

SNAPPS ENGINEERING LTD. built the Schmoozy app. This policy explains how we handle information when you use Schmoozy.

Information Collection and Use

To provide core app functionality, Schmoozy may request access to your camera and contacts. The data required for these features is processed on your device.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not collect personal data beyond what is needed for app operation.

Log Data

If an app error occurs, diagnostic information may be available through Apple platform logs. We do not run our own external tracking system for personal profiling in Schmoozy.

Cookies

Schmoozy does not use browser cookies directly. Some third-party libraries may use local storage mechanisms required for technical operation.

Service Providers

We may use third-party providers that help us deliver app functionality. Those providers only receive limited access required to perform their services.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates are posted on this page with a revised effective date.
