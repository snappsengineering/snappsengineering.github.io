# snappsengineering.github.io

Public **GitHub Pages** site for **SNAPPS ENGINEERING LTD.** app support and privacy policy URLs (e.g. App Store listings).

Live site: **https://snappsengineering.github.io/**

## Privacy (summary)

These pages describe how our apps handle information. We cannot access your app data: we do not operate backends that receive or host your in-app content; your data stays on your device and, where applicable, under your own Apple account (e.g. iCloud).

Full legal text is on each app’s **Privacy Policy** page below.
